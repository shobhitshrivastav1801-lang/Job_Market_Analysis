# 📊 Project Summary

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **742 data science and analytics job postings** to uncover hiring trends, salary patterns, industry demand, company hiring behavior, required technical skills, and the impact of education on salaries. The objective is to provide actionable insights for job seekers, recruiters, and organizations through SQL analysis and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

# 🗄️ Phase 1 – SQL (Data Exploration & Analysis)

The project began with MySQL to explore the dataset, validate data quality, and answer key business questions.

### Activities Performed

- Connected to the MySQL database.
- Explored dataset structure and columns.
- Performed data quality checks.
- Analyzed hiring trends across locations.
- Compared salary distribution by state and job role.
- Identified top hiring industries and companies.
- Analyzed job role demand.
- Evaluated skill requirements by job role.
- Studied the relationship between education and salary.
- Derived business insights and recommendations.

# 📌 Key Insights

- Data Scientist is the most in-demand job role.
- Python and SQL are the most frequently requested technical skills.
- Healthcare & Biotechnology is the leading hiring industry.
- Higher education generally leads to higher average salaries.
- Senior and specialized roles receive significantly better compensation.
- Cloud technologies (AWS) and Big Data tools (Spark) are increasingly valuable.
- Major technology hubs offer the highest number of job opportunities.
- Professionals with multiple technical skills command higher salaries.
